KNDS FRANCE

THE FUTURE OF EUROPEAN LAND SYSTEMS

As the French leader in air-land defense, KNDS France and its subsidiaries support armed forces in meeting the challenges of today and tomorrow.

With its 4,500-strong workforce, KNDS France designs, develops and produces armored vehicles, artillery systems, guns and turrets, ammunition, robots and defense equipment. At the service of its customers, KNDS France is committed to providing tailor-made support throughout the life cycle of their products.


KNDS IN FRANCE

KNDS France is at the heart of the SCORPION program, guaranteeing the production of the GRIFFON and JAGUAR with its partners Arquus and Thales, the SERVAL with Texelis, and the renovation of the Leclerc main battle tank.

In France, KNDS is an architect and industrial prime contractor. Working closely with the French defense procurement agency (DGA), KNDS France guarantees the performance of its products and services, and ensures the smooth operation of its supply chain.



DRIVING INNOVATION IN LAND DEFENSE

With 13% of its turnover dedicated to research and development, innovation is at the heart of KNDS France's missions, providing its customers with a decisive operational advantage.

KNDS France holds more than 2,500 live patents on major themes: weapons and munitions, mobility, protection and stealth, robotics and autonomous systems, artificial intelligence and battlefield digitization, as well as e-training through virtual reality.


OUR STRENGTHS

Combat proven

True force multipliers, KNDS France systems have proven their performance and high level of protection and security in numerous theaters of operation. To date, KNDS France has more than 100 user armies worldwide.

Performance and sustainability

KNDS France is committed to developing high-performance, robust, reliable and durable equipment, adapted to new-generation conflicts and demanding theaters of operation. This commitment also includes initiatives to reduce the environmental footprint of our products.

Customer support

KNDS France offers integrated support services to meet the expectations and anticipate the needs of its customers. Throughout the life cycle of their products, KNDS France provides training and specific operational maintenance services, including the supply of equipment upgrades.

International presence and strategic partnerships

KNDS France benefits from a strong presence in Europe and abroad, and is strengthening its footprint in close collaboration with trusted industrial partners, to share its know-how and contribute to their development.
