WHERE WE ADVENTURE

2 GREAT LOCATIONS IN THE SMOKIES

TOWNSEND, TN

Our Tubing Outposts are located in Townsend, TN - also known as ``The Peaceful Side of the Smokies``. This beautiful, historic mountain community is home to Cades Cove, Foothills Parkway, and one of the Great Smoky Mountain NP's entrances. Townsend is located just 15mi. from Pigeon Forge and 25mi. from Gatlinburg.

HARTFORD, TN

Our Whitewater Outpost is located in Hartford, TN - also known as the “Gateway to the Smokies”. Hartford is one of the most popular whitewater rafting destinations in the country, is near two main Great Smoky Mountain NP entrances, has some of TN’s most stunning mountain scenery, and offers the closest whitewater adventures to both Gatlinburg (25mi.) and Pigeon Forge (30mi.).
