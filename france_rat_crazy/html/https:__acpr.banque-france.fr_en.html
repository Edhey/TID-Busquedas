Discover the ACPR

The Autorité de contrôle prudentiel et de résolution (ACPR – the French Prudential Supervision and Resolution Authority) is an administrative authority that is backed by the Banque de France and responsible for supervising the banking and insurance sectors in France. Find out more about its organisational structure, its strategy and its missions.
