At Mobile World Congress 2025, telecoms industry leaders discussed the future where AI and connectivity come together to tackle today's challenges. Christel Heydemann, CEO of the Orange Group, shares her views on the impact of artificial intelligence in Europe.

Towards 100% high-speed broadband following copper network decommissioning in 162 municipalities

Fiber, being more efficient, sustainable, and better suited to today’s demands, is steadily replacing the copper network that has connected France for over half a century. Following our announcement in late 2019, we’re continuing our gradual closure of this historic network in close partnership with local authorities and public institutions..
