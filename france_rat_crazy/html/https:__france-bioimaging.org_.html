France-BioImaging

We develop, disseminate and give access to cutting-edge technologies and methods in biological imaging.

France-BioImaging is a National Infrastructure in Biology and Health.

AlsaceNODE

Molecular & nanoparticular probes / Tomographic diffractive microscopy

Biomolecules in action / Cell Biology

The Alsace node offer a fully integrated biological imaging portfolio from the molecule to the small animal/plant based on its innovative chemical, optical and biocomputational developments, as well as on its network of state-of-the-art imaging facilities.

ToulouseNODE

Mechanobiology / Molecules and single cells / Whole organisms

The Toulouse node offers 4 fields of expertise: c microscopy, cytometry-cell sorting and data analysis, to visualize animal or plant biological functions from the nanometric scale to the whole organism.

The Toulouse node brings together the T.R.I. Genotoul platform and 8 R&D teams working to offer and develop cutting-edge technologies in the field of mechanobiology, molecular and single cell characterization, whole organism, including intravital imaging and Image analysis, signal treatment and modelisation.

Who are we?

  • FBI provides immediate access to state-of-the-art microscopy techniques and to relevant quantitative measurements to facilitate an integrative understanding of cellular and physiological activities. 
  • FBI brings together, in a single coordinated infrastructure, 30 high-level imaging core facilities supported by world-class associated research and development teams.
  • FBI is the French Node of the European research infrastructure Euro BioImaging and the entry point to access biological imaging services in France.
