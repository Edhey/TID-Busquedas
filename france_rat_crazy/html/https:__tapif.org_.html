NOTE: We do not allow the use of university emails (addresses ending in .edu) in applying to TAPIF as access to these emails is often lost once applicants no longer hold student status. In the past, this has caused communication breakdowns that have resulted in selected assistants not receiving their work contracts on time. In the interest of avoiding these issues, we require for application and participation purposes the use of a personal email that will remain valid through the duration of the TAPIF program in the event that your candidacy is accepted. If accepted, we also highly discourage changing emails during your time as a TAPIF assistant as this can also engender communication issues between assistants and program staff.
